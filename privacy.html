隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

最后更新 / Last Updated: 2026-03-17

概述

订阅管家（SubTracker）尊重并保护您的隐私。本应用不收集、不存储、不传输任何个人信息到外部服务器。

数据存储

您在应用中输入的所有订阅数据均存储在您设备的本地存储中（使用 Apple SwiftData 框架）。这些数据完全由您控制，不会上传到任何服务器。

通知

应用使用 iOS 本地通知功能提醒您即将续费的订阅。通知完全在设备本地处理，不涉及任何外部服务。

第三方服务

本应用不使用任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

数据删除

删除应用即可删除所有数据。您也可以在应用内删除单个订阅记录。

联系我们

如有任何隐私相关问题，请联系：support@subtracker.app

Overview (English)

SubTracker respects and protects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Data Storage

All subscription data you enter is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is entirely under your control and is never uploaded to any server.

Notifications

The app uses iOS local notifications to remind you about upcoming subscription renewals. Notifications are processed entirely on your device with no external services involved.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

Deleting the app will remove all data. You can also delete individual subscription records within the app.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: support@subtracker.app
